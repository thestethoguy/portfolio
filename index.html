Project 03
P2P Credit Risk — Algorithmic Mitigation of Adverse Selection
Economics-grounded ML research pipeline on 2.26M LendingClub loans, rooted in Akerlof's (1970) Market for Lemons theory. Built a Random Forest vs. Logistic Regression credit-screening model with 5-fold stratified cross-validation.

AUC-ROC: 0.709 ± 0.004 — producing 628 fewer false positives per 40K applications, preserving ~$5.3M in capital per screening cycle. Implemented SHAP TreeExplainer with global beeswarm, bar, and per-borrower waterfall plots. Paired with a published 9-section research paper.

Project 04
Global Oil Crisis 2026 — Macroeconomic EDA & Predictive Modelling
End-to-end EDA on the 2026 Strait of Hormuz oil crisis across 3 relational CSVs. Deployed Linear Regression for GDP contraction prediction and Logistic Regression for inflation risk tier classification — mathematically proving South Asian nations suffered the sharpest economic dislocations.

Demonstrated a strong inverse correlation between fuel price surges and stock market stability. Packaged into a publication-ready Jupyter Notebook + exported PDF/HTML report.
